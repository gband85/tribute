Tribute to JSB: Musical Genius

  • 1685: Born in Eisenach, Saxe-Eisenach, Germany
  • 1694: Mother and father died
  • Easter 1707: Christ lag in Todesbanden, one of his first cantatas, is performed
  • 1721: First performance of The Brandenburg Concertos, considered to be some of the best Baroque compositions
  • 1720: Wife died
  • 1723: Appointed Cantor of St. Thomas School in Leipzig
  • 1748-9: Mass in B Minor
  • 1750: Died in Leipzig, Saxony, Germany
